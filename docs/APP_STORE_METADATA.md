## English

### Subtitle
Party question game

### Description

PartyRound is a fun party question game to play with friends.

• Group, confession, and dare questions
• Save players and track parties
• Add custom questions
• Optional scoring or questions-only mode
• Turkish and English

How to play
Pick players, choose question types, turn scoring on or off, and start the party. Questions appear in turn. With scoring on, missed answers or failed dares earn points; with scoring off, just talk it through.

All data stays on your device — no account needed.
